GribOK Privacy Policy

Effective date: March 26, 2026
Developer: Yuri Grib / GribOK

This Privacy Policy describes how GribOK handles information when you use the GribOK mobile application and related messaging, calling and media-sharing features.

1. Information We Process

Depending on how you use the app, GribOK may process the following categories of data:

  • Account and profile information, such as your user ID, display name and avatar.
  • Messages and user content, such as text messages, media, files, group names, group descriptions and related delivery state.
  • Device and service data, such as push notification tokens, app version, basic diagnostics and delivery metadata needed for messaging and calls.
  • Camera and microphone access, when you choose features that require them, such as taking a photo, recording media or joining audio/video calls.
  • Notification-related data used to deliver message and call alerts.

2. Why We Use This Information

GribOK uses the information above to:

  • Send, receive, relay and recover messages, calls and group events.
  • Show your chats, contacts, groups, attachments and message status in the app.
  • Deliver notifications for incoming messages and calls.
  • Maintain service reliability, prevent abuse and troubleshoot technical problems.
  • Support features that you directly request, including camera, microphone and file-sharing features.

3. Android Permissions

GribOK may request Android permissions including the following:

  • Camera to take photos, capture video and support video-related features.
  • Microphone / Record Audio to support audio and video calls, and media capture when you request it.
  • Notifications to alert you about messages and calls.

You can deny permissions, but some features may not work correctly without them.

4. Sharing of Information

GribOK may share or transfer information only as necessary to operate the service, including with infrastructure or service providers that support message delivery, notifications, hosting or security. GribOK is not designed to sell personal data.

5. Data Retention

  • Message and media data stored locally on your device may remain until you delete it, clear app data or uninstall the app.
  • Server-side relay data may be stored temporarily for delivery, retry, notification or recovery purposes and then removed according to service logic.
  • Operational logs and diagnostics may be retained for a limited period for security, abuse prevention and troubleshooting.

6. Data Security

GribOK uses reasonable technical and organizational measures intended to protect information handled by the app. No method of transmission or storage is completely secure, and absolute security cannot be guaranteed.

7. Children's Privacy

GribOK is not intended for children under the age required by applicable law to use the service without parental consent.

8. Your Choices

  • You can manage Android permissions through your device settings.
  • You can disable notifications through app or system settings.
  • You can remove local app data by clearing app storage or uninstalling the app.

9. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. The updated version will be posted at the same URL with a new effective date.
