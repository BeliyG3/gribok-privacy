GribOK Privacy Policy

Effective date: March 27, 2026
Developer: Yuri Grib / GribOK

This Privacy Policy describes how GribOK handles information when you use the GribOK mobile application and its messaging, calling, group, and media features.

GribOK is designed around a profile on this device model. GribOK does not present itself as a traditional recoverable account service with phone-number login or web-based account management. Internal GribOK data can be cleared on the device from inside the app.

1. Information We Process

Depending on how you use GribOK, the app may process the following categories of data:

  • Profile information on this device, such as a generated user identifier, display name, avatar, and local profile settings.
  • Messages and user content, such as text messages, media, files, group titles, group descriptions, and message delivery state.
  • Device and service data, such as push notification tokens, app version, basic diagnostics, and delivery metadata needed for messaging and calling features.
  • Camera and microphone access, when you choose features that require them, such as taking a photo or joining audio/video calls.
  • Notification-related data used to deliver message and call alerts.

2. Why We Use This Information

GribOK uses the information above to:

  • Send, receive, relay, recover, and display messages, calls, and group events.
  • Show your chats, contacts, groups, attachments, and delivery state in the app.
  • Deliver notifications for incoming messages and calls.
  • Maintain service reliability, prevent abuse, and troubleshoot technical problems.
  • Support features that you directly request, including camera, microphone, and file sharing.

3. Android Permissions

GribOK may request Android permissions including the following:

  • Camera for photo capture, video capture, and related features.
  • Microphone / Record Audio for audio and video calls.
  • Notifications for message and call alerts.

You can deny permissions, but some features may not work correctly without them.

4. Clear Profile on This Device

GribOK provides an in-app path to clear internal GribOK data on the current device:

  • Settings > Edit Profile > Clear Profile on This Device

This action is intended to remove internal GribOK data on the current device, including:

  • local chats and message history stored by GribOK on this device;
  • local keys and identity material used by GribOK on this device;
  • cache, temporary data, and profile settings stored by GribOK on this device.

This action does not remove files that you already exported or saved to shared device folders such as Downloads, Pictures, or Movies.

Removing the GribOK app, clearing app storage, or using Clear Profile on This Device removes internal GribOK data on the device. Temporary operational service-side data may still exist for a limited time and is removed automatically over time according to service logic.

5. Sharing of Information

GribOK may use infrastructure or service providers only as necessary to operate messaging, notifications, hosting, security, or delivery and recovery features. GribOK is not designed to sell personal data.

6. Data Retention

  • Internal GribOK data stored on your device may remain until you clear your profile on the device, clear app storage, or uninstall the app.
  • Temporary relay, notification, presence, and operational service data may be stored for delivery, retry, recovery, abuse prevention, or troubleshooting purposes and then removed automatically over time.
  • Information already delivered to other users may remain on their devices according to their own local storage and retention choices.

7. Data Security

GribOK uses reasonable technical and organizational measures intended to protect information handled by the app. No method of transmission or storage is completely secure, and absolute security cannot be guaranteed.

8. Children's Privacy

GribOK is not intended for children under the age required by applicable law to use the service without parental consent.

9. Your Choices

  • You can manage Android permissions through your device settings.
  • You can disable notifications through app or system settings.
  • You can remove internal GribOK data on the current device using Settings > Edit Profile > Clear Profile on This Device.
  • You can also remove internal app data by clearing app storage or uninstalling the app.

10. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. The updated version will be posted at the same URL with a new effective date.
